Final Project

Flappy bird

This program is a recreation of the game called Flappy Bird. The player controls a beaver that moves up and down using the spacebar to avoid obstacles sticks coming from the right side of the screen. Each time the beaver successfully passes through a pair of sticks, the player earns a point. The game ends when the beaver collides with a stick, and the final score is displayed. To play, click the start button on the splash screen, then use the spacebar to control the beaver's movement.
